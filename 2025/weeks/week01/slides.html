What is geomatics?

Geomatics is the discipline concerned with the collection, distribution, storage, analysis, processing, presentation of geographic data or geographic information (ISO 19122:2004)

What is a GIS?

Burrough (1997), defined a GIS as a system for collecting, storing, retrieving at will, transforming and displaying spatial data from the real world for a particular set of purposes.

A working GIS integrates five key components: hardware, software, data, people, and methods
